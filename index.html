Max Menzies

Hello everyone! This site is under construction. I am a postdoctoral fellow at Tsinghua University in Beijing. Previously, I received my PhD in pure maths at Harvard University.

My undergrad degree is a B.A. in mathematics with honors from Cambridge. So I'm familiar with Australia, the UK, the US, and China, and always willing to chat.
